## How to Use

Once you pick a template:

1. Copy the code from `ThreeScene-Templates.jsx` (the template you want)
2. Replace the entire contents of `ThreeScene.jsx` with it
3. Save and refresh your browser
4. The scene will appear behind your hero text

---

## Customization Tips

Each template is fully customizable:

- **Colors**: Change hex values like `#00ff88` to any color you want
- **Speed**: Adjust rotation amounts (smaller = slower, bigger = faster)
- **Size**: Modify `scale={1}` to make objects bigger/smaller
- **Intensity**: Change `emissiveIntensity={0.8}` for brighter/dimmer glow
